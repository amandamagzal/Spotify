   Hello :)

   In this project, we create different visualizations to explore the relationship between the popularity, genre and emotions expressed in songs.

   The dataset we used contains all the songs in Spotify's Daily Top 200 charts in 35+1 (global) countries around the world for a period of over 3 years (2017-2020).

   The popularity is calculated as follows:
   A score from 1 to 200 is assigned to each song. #1 Ranked gets 200, #2 ranked gets 199, â€¦ , #200 ranked gets 1.
   Then, it's multiplied by a modificator which is 3 for #1 2.2 for the #2 1.7 for the #3 1.3 for #4-10 1 for # 11-50 0.85 for #51-100 0.8 for #101-200 for every day
   The daily score for a SAME song in a SAME country is summed (same song in different country has different popularity).

   The emotion score is calculated as follows:
   Number of words related to the emotion divided by the total number of words found by the dictionary.


    Visualization #1:

   When you select a genre from the index, the radar chart presents the average score of each emotion for the chosen genre, and the barplot presents its average popularity score.

    Visualization #2:

   When you select a country on the map, the boxplot presents the distribution of each emotion for the chosen country. The color of the country corresponds to the most popular genre.

    Visualization #3:

   When you select an emotion from the combo box, a word cloud is created with the top 15 songs for the selected emotion.
   The color of the title corresponds to its genre. The bigger the title, the higher it is ranked.
   Click on a title to listen on Spotify!
